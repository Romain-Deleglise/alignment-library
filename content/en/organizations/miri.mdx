# MIRI (Machine Intelligence Research Institute)

## Mission

Research mathematical foundations needed to ensure smarter-than-human AI has positive impact.

## Founded

2000 (as Singularity Institute)

## Key People

- Eliezer Yudkowsky (founder, researcher)
- Nate Soares (Executive Director)
- Rob Bensinger (communications)

## Research Focus

- Agent foundations
- Embedded agency
- Decision theory
- Logical uncertainty

## Key Publications

- [Risks from Learned Optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/1906.01820)
- [Embedded Agency](https://intelligence.org/2018/10/29/embedded-agency/)
- [Agent Foundations Agenda](https://intelligence.org/files/TechnicalAgenda.pdf)

## Perspective

- High P(doom) (~90%+)
- Alignment is very difficult
- Current approaches insufficient
- Need fundamental breakthroughs
